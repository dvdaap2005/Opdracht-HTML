Black Widow

Black Widow, the elite spy and master assassin, brings her agility, stealth, and deadly combat skills to Marvel Rivals.
Equipped with her Widow’s Bite electroshock gauntlets and expert hand-to-hand combat abilities, she excels in both close-quarters combat and high-speed tactical maneuvers.

In the game, Black Widow forms the "Allied Agents" team-up with Hawkeye.
With his Hunter’s Sight, she gains enhanced vision, allowing her to track enemy afterimages and deal increased damage.
This synergy makes her a lethal hunter, perfect for flanking enemies and executing precise eliminations.

With her stealth, speed, and deadly precision, Black Widow is an excellent choice for players who enjoy outmaneuvering their opponents and striking when they least expect it.
Whether infiltrating enemy lines or working alongside Hawkeye, she proves why she is one of the most feared spies in the Marvel universe.
